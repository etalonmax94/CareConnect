application for managing clients in an NDIS, Aged Care (Home Care Packages - HCP), and Private clients business. The app should serve as a CRM system with client profiles, document management, budgeting, and funds tracking features. Use the following structure based on my existing Excel data structure (with sheets for NDIS, SaH/Aged Care, Private, and Archive):
Main Modules:

Clients Module (Central form for all clients, with a 'Client Type' field to categorize as NDIS, Aged Care, Private, or Archive. This allows unified management with type-specific fields shown conditionally.)
Key Fields:
Client Name (Text, Required)
Care Manager (Lookup to a separate Care Managers module or Text)
Leadership/Clinical Notes (Multi-line Text)
Photo (Image Upload)
DOB (Date)
Age (Formula: Calculate automatically from DOB)
Home Address (Address Field)
Phone Number (Phone)
Schedule & Arrival Notification (Multi-line Text, e.g., SMS/Call preferences)
NOK/EPOA (Multi-line Text, e.g., Next of Kin details)
Email (Email)
Medicare Number (Text)
Frequency of Services (Multi-line Text)
Main Diagnosis (Multi-line Text)
Summary of Services Received (Multi-line Text)
Communication Needs (Text)
Funding Details (Subform or Section):
For NDIS: NDIS Number (Text), Funding Type (Picklist: Plan, Agency), Plan Start Date (Date), Plan End Date (Date)
For Aged Care: HCP Number (Text), HCP Funding Level (Picklist: Level 1, Level 2, Level 3, Level 4)
For Private: Funding Source (Text, e.g., QLD Health, Self-Funded)

Service Agreement Date (Date, Annual Renewal)
Schedule of Supports/Budget (Currency or Subform for budget breakdown)
Consent Form Date (Date, Annual)
NDIS Consent Form Date (Date, Conditional for NDIS)
Risk Assessment Date (Date, Annual)
Self Assessment of Medication Tool (Date)
Medication Consent Date (Date, Annual)
Personal Emergency Management (Date)
Care Plan Date (Date, 6-monthly)
Health Summary Date (Date, 6-monthly)
Wound Care Plan (Multi-line Text)
General Practitioner (Subform: Name, Clinic, Phone, Email)
Support Coordinator (Text, for NDIS/Aged Care)
Plan Manager (Text)
Other Health Professionals (Multi-line Text)
Allied Health Reports (File Upload)
High Intensity Supports Provided (Multi-line Text, e.g., IDC/SPC/PEG)
Intake Form/Initial Assessment (File Upload)
Service Delivery Record (Subform or Lookup to Records module)
Progress Notes (Subform: Date, Note, Author)
Money and Property Form (File Upload)
Referrals Made (Multi-line Text)
Plan Reviews (Subform: Date, Notes)
Incidents (Subform: Date, Description, Resolution)
Complaints/Feedback (Subform: Date, Details)
Invoices (Lookup to Invoices module or Subform)


Budgeting and Funds Tracking Module (Linked to Clients via Client ID):
Track funding allocation, usage, and remaining balance.
Fields:
Client Lookup (Required)
Budget Category (Picklist: e.g., ADLs, Community Access, Nursing, Travel/Transport)
Total Allocated Budget (Currency)
Start Date (Date)
End Date (Date)
Used Amount (Currency, Calculated from linked services/invoices)
Remaining Balance (Formula: Total Allocated - Used Amount)
Funding Alerts (Workflow: Email reminders 30 days before plan end or when balance <20%)

Subform for Transactions: Date, Service Type, Amount, Invoice Reference.

Invoices Module (For tracking billing and payments, linked to Clients and Budgets):
Fields:
Client Lookup
Invoice Date (Date)
Service Provided (Multi-line Text)
Amount (Currency)
Payment Status (Picklist: Pending, Paid, Overdue)
Linked Budget (Lookup)
Invoice Upload (File)

Workflow: Auto-update client's budget usage on invoice approval.

Care Managers/Support Coordinators Module (Simple directory for lookups):
Fields: Name, Email, Phone, Organization.

Documents/Attachments Module (Central repository linked to Clients):
Fields: Client Lookup, Document Type (Picklist: Consent, Care Plan, Reports, etc.), Upload Date, File.


Additional Features:

Workflows and Automations:
Send email/SMS reminders for expiring dates (e.g., Plan End, Risk Assessment, Care Plan - 30/7 days prior).
Auto-calculate age and budget balances.
Approval workflows for new clients or budget changes.
Archive workflow: Move inactive clients to 'Archive' status.

Reports and Dashboards:
Client Overview Dashboard: Charts for active clients by type, upcoming renewals, low budgets.
Funds Usage Report: By client, category, or period.
Compliance Report: Overdue assessments/consents.
Custom Views: Filtered by Client Type (NDIS, Aged Care, Private).

Security and Access:
Role-based: Admin (full access), Care Managers (view/edit own clients), Nurses (add notes/records).
Data Import: Allow importing from Excel (match fields to my provided sheet structure).

Integrations:
Integrate with Zoho CRM for leads/prospects.
Email/SMS for notifications.
Calendar for scheduling visits.
