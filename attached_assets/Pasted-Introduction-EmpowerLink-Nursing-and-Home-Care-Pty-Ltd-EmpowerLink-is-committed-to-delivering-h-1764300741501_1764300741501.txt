Introduction

EmpowerLink Nursing and Home Care Pty Ltd (“EmpowerLink”) is committed to delivering high-quality, person-centred care that meets your individual health needs. To ensure safe and effective service provision, we require access to relevant personal and health information.

We recognise the sensitive nature of your personal and health information. All information collected is treated with the highest level of confidentiality and is managed in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988, the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), and Queensland’s health privacy legislation.

Scope of Consent

By providing your consent, you explicitly authorise EmpowerLink to collect, store, use, and disclose your personal and health information for the purpose of providing safe and effective care. Specifically, you consent to:

The collection and secure storage of your personal and health information, including medical history, diagnoses, treatment plans, medications, and other relevant healthcare details.
The use of your information for developing, reviewing, and implementing your care plan, coordinating services, monitoring progress, and ensuring continuity and quality of care.
The disclosure of your information to healthcare professionals, care providers, and relevant personnel directly involved in your care, including but not limited to doctors, nurses, allied health professionals, and case managers.
The secure storage of your information in compliance with Australian healthcare security standards, including electronic health records with controlled access.
Information Security and Privacy

EmpowerLink has implemented stringent measures to protect the security and confidentiality of your information. These measures include:

Secure electronic storage systems with industry-standard encryption and restricted access.
Mandatory privacy and confidentiality training for all staff handling personal information.
Strict access controls ensuring only authorised personnel can view or use your information.
Regular audits and compliance reviews to maintain the highest data security standards.
Your Rights

Under Australian privacy laws, you have the right to:

Access your personal information held by EmpowerLink.
Request corrections to any inaccuracies, incomplete, or outdated information.
Withdraw or modify your consent at any time by providing written notice to EmpowerLink. However, please be advised that withdrawing your consent may limit the scope of services we can provide, as certain information is required for safe and effective care.
Client Photo and Media Consent

To enhance the accuracy of identification and ensure the safety of our services, we request your consent to take and securely store a photograph of you. Additionally, EmpowerLink may invite you to participate in media materials, including photographs, videos, or testimonials, to help promote and improve our services. Your participation in these materials is entirely voluntary.

By providing your consent, you agree that:

Your photo will be securely stored in our system and accessed only by authorised personnel for identification purposes.
The photo will not be shared with third parties unless required by law or with your explicit consent.
You may be invited to participate in media materials (photos, videos, testimonials), but participation is voluntary.
You may withdraw your consent at any time in writing, and your photo or media materials will be securely deleted from our records.
Client's Money and Property Management (optional)

By providing your consent below, you authorise EmpowerLink and its designated support workers to assist with limited money and property management in accordance with EmpowerLink’s Financial Management and Client Property Policy, the NDIS (Code of Conduct) Rules 2018, and Aged Care Act 1997. Specifically, you consent to:

Support workers using your bank card or cash to make purchases only for agreed-upon items or services directly related to your care and wellbeing.
Support workers providing itemised receipts and returning any remaining change or funds after each transaction.
EmpowerLink maintaining accurate financial transaction records, including purpose, amount, and evidence of each purchase, within your file.
EmpowerLink conducting regular audits and supervision to ensure your funds and property are managed safely and ethically.
EmpowerLink staff are not permitted to use a client’s funds for any personal reason or without a clear, documented purpose authorised by EmpowerLink and the client (or their authorised representative). You retain full control over your finances and may revoke or amend this consent at any time in writing.
